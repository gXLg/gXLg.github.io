Possible parameters

max: mx[Int]_my[Int]; mx - Screen width, my - Screen height

rebound: r[0-100]; The percentage of how much velocity remains when hitting on the border

show: s[0,1]; Whether to show the weights on the planet

prints: pr[0,1]; Whether to draw prints behind planets

ground: g[Number]; Acceleration of the ground line, as if it would be a big planet

planets: p[\-.0-9a-zA-Z]; Special objects string, generated when sharing a situation
